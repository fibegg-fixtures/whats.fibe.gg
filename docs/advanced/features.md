Account-wide preferences and per-browser interface tuning.

## Account-wide preferences

### Teams (Collaboration)

Unlocks creating and managing [Teams](/concepts/teams/). Off by default and enabled per account rather than from this page. Without it the Teams page is hidden — you can still accept a team invitation and use a Marquee shared with you.

### Beta program

Fibe runs as a beta: signing in and authenticating against the [API](/api/) require beta participation, granted per account. Without it, sign-in redirects to the waitlist.

## Per-browser preferences

Stored on this browser only. Don't affect other devices.

### Animations and visual effects

Turn off on slow devices. Disables decorative motion, smooth scrolling, blur, and glow effects. UI remains functional.

Two states surfaced:

- **Full visual effects enabled** — default.
- **Reduced effects active on this device** — set when off.
